Who are we?

SKP Company, formed by two industry experts, is named after the Skopje airport code. We specialize in international transport, logistics, and forwarding, focusing on customs representation for import- export processes. Our main activities involve facilitating communication with state services, obtaining permits, and handling documentation for shipped goods.
